Privacy

We respect your privacy. Basic app data may be stored in your browser (localStorage) and, if you choose to sign in, in Supabase under your account. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

What We Store

  • Local (PC Cache): Work sessions, settings, and todos saved in your browser.
  • Cloud (Supabase): If logged in, sessions, settings, and todos saved to your account.
  • Feedback: Messages you submit via the footer form.

Data Use

  • Data is used to provide app functionality and improve the product.
  • No selling or sharing with third parties.
  • You can delete your data from within the app (sessions/todos) at any time.
